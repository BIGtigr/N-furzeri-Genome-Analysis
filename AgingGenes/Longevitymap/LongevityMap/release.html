LongevityMap Build 1 Release Notes

Release date:July 26, 2013
Number of entries: 504
Number of genes: 751
Number of variants: 1987

This is the first build of the LongevityMap, a database of human genetic variants associated with longevity. Negative results are also included in the LongevityMap and data was manually curated from 246 genetic studies of human longevity.

Please remember these notes refer to the latest stable build of the LongevityMap. The current LongevityMap stats may differ slightly from the above due to changes after the build's release.

More information concerning the design and curation of the LongevityMap is available elsewhere. Use of the LongevityMap is free but subject to the conditions of the Human Ageing Genomic Resources. To cite the LongevityMap please use:

Budovsky, A., Craig, T., Wang, J., Tacutu, R., Csordas, A., Lourenco, J., Fraifeld, V. E., de Magalhaes, J. P. (in press) "LongevityMap: A database of human genetic variants associated with longevity." Trends in Genetics (DOI: 10.1016/j.tig.2013.08.003).
